Quick Setup Guide

1

Get Your Bot Token

Message @BotFather on Telegram and create a new bot. You'll receive a bot token like:

8395607834:AAE7IJEt1xVs4-WzJxcntAfMES3IcpRnjtg
2

Get Your Admin ID

Message @userinfobot on Telegram. It will reply with your User ID:

6012422087
3

Deploy to Heroku

Click the "Deploy to Heroku" button. Enter your credentials when prompted. The bot is already pre-configured with:

BOT_TOKEN: 8395607834:AAE7IJEt1xVs4-WzJxcntAfMES3IcpRnjtg ADMIN_ID: 6012422087

You can change these to your own credentials.

4

Start Using Your Bot

Once deployed, go to your Telegram bot and send /start. You're now ready to manage support tickets!

Important Notes

• The bot comes pre-configured with sample credentials
• Replace with your own BOT_TOKEN for security
• Heroku free tier: 550 hours/month (enough for continuous operation)
• Bot may sleep after 30 minutes of inactivity
